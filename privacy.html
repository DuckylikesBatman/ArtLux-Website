Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 7, 2025

At ArtLux, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website, how we use it, and the choices you have. If you have any questions, contact us at artluxdesign@outlook.com.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide via forms. When you use the Contact or Commission forms, we collect the details you submit (name, email, phone, project details). These forms are processed by Formspree.
  • Basic technical data. Our servers may log basic metadata such as IP address and browser type for security and reliability.
  • Cookies & analytics. We currently do not use third-party analytics or advertising cookies on this site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To reply to your inquiries and commission requests
  • To prepare quotations and project documentation
  • To maintain website security and reliability

Legal Bases

We process your information on the following bases:

  • Contractual necessity to respond to commission requests
  • Legitimate interests for website security
  • Consent when you voluntarily submit a form

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Service providers. We use Formspree to receive form submissions
  • Legal requirements. If required by law or to protect our rights

Data Retention

We retain form submissions for as long as necessary to respond to your request and manage our relationship, and to comply with legal obligations. You can ask us to delete your message at any time.

Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at artluxdesign@outlook.com.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Children

Our website is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If changes are significant, we will adjust the "Last updated" date and provide notice where appropriate.
