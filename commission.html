Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commission typically take?

Most commissions take 4–6 weeks after concept approval. Larger or complex works may require 8–10 weeks.

What materials do you use?

We use archival‑quality media and supports, including:

  • Professional acrylics and oils
  • Gallery‑wrapped canvases
  • Acid‑free papers and boards
  • UV‑protective varnishes
Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, worldwide shipping with custom crating, full insurance, and tracked delivery.
